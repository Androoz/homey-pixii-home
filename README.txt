Use Pixii Home with Homey Pro to monitor battery level, power, stored energy and operating status, and to include your battery in energy-saving Flows. Charge, discharge, maintain a target battery level or dynamically balance grid import and export through the local MQTT connection to your Pixii Gateway.

This independent community app is not an official Pixii or Athom integration. It communicates locally with the MQTT broker configured in your Pixii Gateway and requires no developer-operated cloud service.

Pixii documents Broker 2 as the typical choice for an external broker and Broker 1 as the local/onboard broker. Use Broker 2 first for Homey and ensure that only one connected system publishes battery control commands.
